About Me

Hi, I'm lonelypigeon1711! I am a senior at Brown University concentrating in both Computer Science and Visual Art. After completing several big tech internships, I am looking for ways to merge my studies and make computer science more creative. I'm trying to look at where art and computer science overlap, and see how I can be impactful in those spaces. Some of that includes my exploration of animation, but also by diving into UI/UX I hoped to gain perspective of design practices currently in the CS community. In my free time, I enjoy being outside, roller-skating, and making Spotify playlists!
